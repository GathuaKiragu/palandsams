About Us

Company History

Pal and Sams Limited was founded in 2021 and for over 3 years, we have reliably supplied and installed fully integrated security solutions including CCTV Cameras, electric fences and concertina (razor) wire systems, access control systems, alarm systems and automatic gates among others.

We are able to protect everything from small offices, homes and schools to large multinational organizations and high security government facilities.

Our Vision

To be the leading security technology company in Eastern and Central Africa.

Our Mission

To deliver the best, efficient and affordable security and technology solutions in the markets we serve.
